Privacy Policy

Last updated: 06/23/2026

Yazia ("we", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how you can stay in control of it while using our app.

01

Information We Collect

  • Account information: your email address, and a password stored in encrypted form
  • Personal journal: the content you write is stored only on your device (local storage). It is never transmitted to our servers, and we never read or analyze it
  • Usage data: basic technical information (screens viewed, in-app interactions, device type) to improve the experience
Good to know

Because your journal is stored locally, it is not automatically backed up: if you uninstall the app or switch devices without exporting your entries beforehand, this content will be lost permanently.

Coming soon

When you enable location-based reminders, we will collect your geographic location solely to trigger those reminders at the appropriate moment. You will be able to disable this feature at any time.

02

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Offer you a personal reflection space you can return to over time
  • Communicate important information related to your account
  • Keep the app secure
03

Sharing of Information

We never sell your personal data. We may share information only with trusted technical providers who help us operate the app, and only for that purpose:

  • Supabase (hosting, database, authentication)
  • Google Analytics for Firebase (anonymized audience measurement: screens viewed, in-app interactions)

We may disclose information where required by law.

04

Data Security

Your data is hosted through Supabase, which encrypts data in transit and at rest. We take reasonable steps to protect your information, but no system is entirely infallible — we encourage you to use a strong, unique password.

05

Your Choices

You can, at any time:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete an individual journal entry
  • Delete your account entirely, which permanently erases your associated data

To exercise these rights, contact us at soulbloom.appli@gmail.com.

06

Children's Privacy

Yazia is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information without parental consent, please contact us so we can remove it.

07

International Users

By using Yazia, you agree that your information may be processed and stored through our providers (Supabase, Google/Firebase), regardless of your country of residence.

08

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically, particularly when new features are added (for example, location-based reminders). Any significant change will be communicated to you through the app or by email. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
